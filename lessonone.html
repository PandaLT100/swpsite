Formalities

Spanish has 2 forms of speech. Casual and Formal. The two forms of speech are used to address “You” in two different ways.


When do you use each form of speech?

Casual Speech - this form of speech is used when you want to address someone with you who is your age, younger than you, a friend, family, etc. To put it simple, you use casual speech with people you are familiar with.

Formal Speech - this form of speech is used when you want to address someone with you who is older than you, someone in higher rank than you (like a boss), or strangers.


There is also informal speech, which can be seen as more casual versions of normal words. An english example may be like saying “Sup” instead of “Hello”

Greetings

Hello

Hola - Hello

Buenos dias - Good morning

Buenas tardes - good afternoon

Buenas noches - good night

Hasta pronto - until next time, see you soon (Lit. Until Soon)

Hasta luego - Until next time

Hasta manana - See you tomorrow/Good night


How Are You?

¿Cómo estás? - How are you?

¿Cómo está? - How are you? (Formal)

¿Cómo estan (ustedes)? - How are you guys?


Good. And You?

Bien, y tu? - Good, and you?

(Yo) estoy ___ - I am ___

Bien - Good

Mal - Bad

Más o menos - More or less, So-so

Y tú? - And you?

Y usted? - And you? (formal)


How Are you?

¿Cómo te llamas? - What's your name?

Me llamo___ - My name is___ (Lit. I am called…)

Mi nombre es ___ - My name is ___ (Lit. I am called...)


Please And Thank You

Por favor - Please

Gracias - Thank you

De nada - You're welcome, No problem

No hay problema - No problem, You're welcome


A Pleasure

Un placer conocerlo/conocerla - A pleasure to meet you (formal)

Un placer conocerte - A pleasure to meet you

Un placer - A pleasure

Mucho gusto - Nice to meet you

A usted tambien - You too (formal)

A ti tambien - You too


Goodbye

Hasta luego/ hasta la vista - See you later

Hasta la proxima - Until next time

Nos vemos despues - See you later, we'll see each other later

Hasta mañana - See you tomorrow, goodnight

Adios - Goodbye

Chau - Goodbye (informal)
